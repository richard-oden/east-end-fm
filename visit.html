Where

13060 Factory Lane, Louisville, KY 40245

We're located in the parking lot of Grace Evangelical Church, across the street from the Forest Springs Kroger/Shopping Center and next door to the Middletown Fire Department.

Just minutes away from the Ford Truck Plant, Middletown, Anchorage, and Crestwood!

Directions from I-265:

  1. Get off I-265 at Exit 30 Lagrange Road, KY-146.
  2. Take a right off the exit onto Lagrange Road.
  3. Take a right at the first light onto Factory Lane.
  4. The farmer's market is just .3 miles down Factory Lane next to the Middletown Fire Department on the Right.

Just look for the farmer's market signs!
